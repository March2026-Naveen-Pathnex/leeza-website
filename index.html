Our Story

Crafting Joy,
One Scoop at a Time

Leeza was born from a simple dream — to bring the purest, most joyful ice cream experience to the streets of Palwal. What started as a small family kitchen experiment has blossomed into a beloved destination where every flavor tells a story.

We believe ice cream is more than just a dessert. It's a warm memory, a celebration, a reason to smile. That's why we craft each batch with premium ingredients, traditional techniques, and a whole lot of heart.
